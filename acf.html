About Us

Meta's AI & Compute Foundation team, led by Chunqiang Tang (a.k.a. CQ Tang), comprises world-class research scientists and engineers developing foundational AI and compute technologies. Our focus areas include:

  • Drive Meta's strategy for hardware innovation: We define and execute the company's strategy for new hardware technologies, spanning Meta's homegrown silicon---AI chips called MTIA, custom ARM CPUs, and CXL ASICs---as well as vendor-supplied GPUs, CPUs, memory, and storage.
  • Productionize innovation through co-design: We translate innovation into real-world impact by co-designing across every layer of the stack---from silicon to software to AI models---creating integrated solutions that redefine performance at scale.
  • Build AI software to serve billions: We develop the complete software ecosystem for Meta's homegrown AI chips, ranging from compilers and kernels to large-scale LLM inference and training. We also build foundational software technologies for GPUs and CPUs.

Our primary measure of success is delivering AI and compute systems into production to serve billions of users. This requires continuous innovation, and our philosophy is research in production: first delivering production systems with broad impact, then summarizing our innovations and experiences as research papers at top conferences. We have won Best Paper Awards at prestigious conferences such as ISCA, ASPLOS, SOSP, and OSDI, with multiple papers selected for IEEE Micro Top Picks. We also publish in broader venues such as ICML, NeurIPS, SC, HPCA, NSDI, VLDB, and MLSys. While we value our strong publication record, we take even greater pride in the global impact of the production systems we build for billions of people.
